About Me

Hi, I’m Shelby, a mental health therapy intern passionate about using research-backed therapeutic modalities to help clients work through their mental health concerns. I employ Person-Centered Theory, CBT, SB-CBT, Existential Therapy, Narrative Exposure Therapy, and Positive Psychology, tailoring my approach to the unique needs of each of my clients. I help with a variety of concerns, including:

  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Autism
  • OCD
  • Phobias
  • Sexual Concerns
  • Relationship Concerns
  • Trauma (Simple & Complex)
